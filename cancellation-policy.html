Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

At DnA Financial, we strive to provide high-quality bookkeeping and financial organization services. This policy outlines our procedures for cancellations and refunds.

Cancellation of Monthly Bookkeeping Services

Clients may cancel monthly bookkeeping services at any time by providing written notice via email to info@dnafinancial.org. Cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing month. Services already rendered for that month are non-refundable, and no further charges will be billed after the cancellation date.

Cancellation of Catch-Up & Cleanup Projects

These are project-based services with the following cancellation terms:

  • Before Work Begins: If a client cancels before any bookkeeping work has started, a full refund of any deposit or prepaid fees will be issued.
  • After Work Begins: If work has already commenced, the client will be billed for the hours or portions of the project completed up to the point of cancellation. Any remaining balance from a prepaid deposit will be refunded.

Refunds

Refunds for monthly bookkeeping services are not provided for partial months. For project-based services, refunds are processed based on the status of the work as described above. All refund requests must be made in writing.

Non-Refundable Items

The following fees are strictly non-refundable:

  • Late submission fees ($10.00 flat fee)
  • Late payment fees (5% of outstanding balance)
  • Returned check or declined payment fees
  • Any other administrative fees incurred

How to Cancel

To initiate a cancellation, please send an email to info@dnafinancial.org with your full name and business name. We will confirm receipt and process your request promptly.
