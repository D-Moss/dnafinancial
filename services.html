Services

Bookkeeping services designed to keep your records organized.

DnA Financial provides monthly bookkeeping, catch-up bookkeeping, and financial record cleanup for small businesses.

Monthly Bookkeeping

Monthly bookkeeping helps keep your financial records organized throughout the year.

  • Review bank and credit card activity
  • Categorize income and expenses
  • Track unclear transactions
  • Prepare monthly bookkeeping summaries
  • Maintain tax-ready records

Catch-Up Bookkeeping

Catch-up bookkeeping is for businesses that are behind and need past months organized.

  • Review prior statements
  • Organize historical transactions
  • Identify missing information
  • Prepare catch-up summaries
  • Provide a custom quote after review

Financial Record Cleanup

Cleanup services help organize existing spreadsheets, records, and reports that need correction or structure.

  • Review current records
  • Correct inconsistent categories
  • Organize supporting documents
  • Track unresolved items
  • Prepare cleaner records for tax time
